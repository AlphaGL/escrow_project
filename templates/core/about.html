About SafeRelease Nigeria

Building trust in every transaction across Nigeria by protecting both clients and service providers through secure escrow payments.

Our Mission

Making online service payments safe, secure, and stress-free for all Nigerians

Our Mission

To make online service payments safe, secure, and stress-free for all Nigerians. We believe that trust is the foundation of every successful transaction. That's why we've built a platform that protects both clients and service providers, ensuring that money only changes hands when work is completed satisfactorily.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted payment platform for service transactions, enabling thousands of businesses and freelancers to work with confidence. We envision a future where payment disputes are rare, transactions are seamless, and trust is the default between clients and service providers.

Why We Built SafeRelease

We saw real problems affecting real people and decided to solve them

The Problem

  • Freelancers getting scammed after delivering quality work
  • Clients paying upfront and receiving poor quality or no work at all
  • No trusted intermediary to handle payments fairly
  • Disputes with no clear or fair resolution process
  • Fear and hesitation preventing valuable transactions

Our Solution

  • Secure escrow holds money until work is completed and approved
  • Both parties are protected throughout the entire process
  • Clear communication channels and dispute resolution system
  • Transparent fees with no hidden charges or surprises
  • Automated processes that ensure fairness and efficiency
