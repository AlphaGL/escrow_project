About TrustEscrow Nigeria

Building trust in every transaction across Nigeria

Our Mission

To make online service payments safe, secure, and stress-free for all Nigerians.

We believe that trust is the foundation of every successful transaction. That's why we've built a platform that protects both clients and service providers, ensuring that money only changes hands when work is completed satisfactorily.

Why We Built TrustEscrow

The Problem

  • Freelancers getting scammed after delivering work
  • Clients paying upfront and getting poor quality or no work
  • No trusted way to handle payments for services
  • Disputes with no fair resolution process

Our Solution

  • Secure escrow holds money until work is done
  • Both parties are protected throughout the process
  • Clear communication and dispute resolution
  • Transparent fees and automated processes
