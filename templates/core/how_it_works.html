How SafeRelease Works

Simple, secure, and transparent escrow payments in 3 easy steps

1

Client Pays into Escrow

Client creates a transaction and pays the agreed amount. Money is held securely in SafeRelease, not released to the provider yet.

  • Money is completely safe
  • Work can now begin
  • Provider is instantly notified
  • Full payment protection
2

Provider Completes Work

Service provider accepts the job and completes the work as agreed. Both parties can communicate securely through the platform.

  • Provider starts working
  • Secure messaging system
  • Work progress tracking
  • Final work submission
3

Payment Released

Client reviews the completed work. If satisfied, they approve and payment is released. Or payment auto-releases after 5 days with no dispute.

  • Client reviews work
  • Approval or auto-release
  • Provider gets paid instantly
  • Transaction complete!

The Complete Process

For Clients (Buyers)

  1. Create an account and verify your email address
  2. Create a new transaction with detailed service requirements
  3. Enter the provider's email or phone number
  4. Pay the agreed amount via card, bank transfer, or USSD
  5. Money is held securely in escrow - completely safe
  6. Communicate with provider through secure messaging
  7. Review the completed work thoroughly
  8. Approve payment or raise a dispute if unsatisfied
  9. Leave a rating to help other users

For Service Providers (Sellers)

  1. Create an account and add your bank details
  2. Receive notification of new transaction via email/SMS
  3. Review job details and requirements carefully
  4. Accept the job to confirm you'll do the work
  5. Start working on the project with confidence
  6. Communicate with client for clarifications
  7. Complete the work to the best of your ability
  8. Mark work as completed for client review
  9. Receive payment directly to your bank account
