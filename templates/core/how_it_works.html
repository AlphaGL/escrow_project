How TrustEscrow Works

Simple, secure, and transparent escrow payments in 3 easy steps

Client Pays into Escrow

Client creates a transaction and pays the agreed amount. Money is held securely in TrustEscrow, not released to the provider yet.

  • Money is safe
  • Work can now begin
  • Provider is notified

Provider Completes Work

Service provider accepts the job and completes the work as agreed. Both parties can communicate through the platform.

  • Provider starts work
  • Secure messaging
  • Work submitted

Payment Released

Client reviews the completed work. If satisfied, they approve and payment is released. Or payment auto-releases after 5 days.

  • Client approves
  • Auto-release option
  • Provider gets paid

The Complete Process

For Clients (Buyers)

  1. Create an account and verify your details
  2. Create a new transaction with service details
  3. Enter provider's email or phone number
  4. Pay the amount via card, bank transfer, or USSD
  5. Money is held in secure escrow
  6. Communicate with provider through messages
  7. Review completed work
  8. Approve payment or raise dispute if needed

For Service Providers (Sellers)

  1. Create an account and add bank details
  2. Receive notification of new transaction
  3. Review job details and accept
  4. Start working on the project
  5. Communicate with client for clarifications
  6. Complete work and mark as done
  7. Wait for client approval (max 5 days)
  8. Receive payment directly to your bank account
